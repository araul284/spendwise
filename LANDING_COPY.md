## FAQ — 5 real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**

A: Completely free, no credit card, no account. SpendWise is a lead-generation tool for Credex — if your audit shows significant savings potential, we'll suggest a Credex consultation where you can get discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your stack is already optimised, we tell you that honestly and you never hear from us again unless you want to.

---

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**

A: We verify every number from official vendor pricing pages and note the date we checked. Pricing changes — we recommend re-running your audit quarterly. Every data point in our audit engine has a source URL in our public PRICING_DATA.md file. If you spot something wrong, email us.

---

**Q: Do I need to connect my accounts or share API keys?**

A: No. You manually input what you pay and how many seats you have. We never connect to your billing, your Stripe, or your bank. There's nothing to authorise and no credentials to share.

---

**Q: What do you do with my data?**

A: If you submit your email, we store it (along with your audit results) to send you the report and follow up if your savings are significant. We don't sell your data. Your audit results can be shared via a public link — that version strips your email and company name, showing only tool names and savings numbers.

---

**Q: What if my tool isn't on the list?**

A: We currently support Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf — the eight tools that account for the vast majority of AI spend at startups. If you're paying for something not on this list, you can note it in the optional fields when capturing your report and we'll look at adding it.
